I’m a PhD student at the Jožef Stefan International Postgraduate School in Ljubljana, Slovenia. My academic interests lie primarily in machine learning, but I also enjoy mathematics and computer science.

I'm from Skopje, North Macedonia, where I finished elementary school (OOU Gjorgjija Pulevski) and high school (SUGS Rade Jovchevski - Korchagin). After finishing high school, I moved to Slovenia to study at the University of Ljubljana. I got my bachelor’s degree in computer science and mathematics from the Faculty of Computer and Information Science and my master’s degree in computer science and mathematics at the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics.

In my free time I like listening to music and playing video games. I love talking about music I'm listening to and games I've been playing so feel free to ask me about any of those.
